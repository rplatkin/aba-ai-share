AI in the Trenches and on the Bench

Source Materials

ABA Business Law Section Spring Meeting 2026 | Atlanta
Compiled by Hon. Richard Platkin

This collection curates the source materials referenced in the ABA Business Law Section panel discussion "AI in the Trenches and on the Bench." The materials span landmark court decisions on AI misconduct and privilege, policy frameworks from state and federal judiciaries, practitioner analyses, and foundational research on AI capabilities and limitations.

All files are organized by topic for quick reference during and after the panel. Links open directly in your browser or download to your device.
