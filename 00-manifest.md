### 31. `2509.04664v1.pdf`
- **Format:** PDF | **Size:** 796 KB | **Pages:** ~20

- **Summary:** "Why Language Models Hallucinate" by Adam Tauman Kalai, Ofir Nachum, Santosh S. Vempala, and Edwin Zhang (OpenAI / Georgia Tech), arXiv Sept. 4, 2025. Theoretical analysis providing a mathematical framework for why LLMs hallucinate. Two structural causes: (1) pretraining on corpora containing errors statistically guarantees error reproduction; (2) post-training evaluation benchmarks reward guessing over admitting uncertainty ("I don't know"), creating systematic pressure to hallucinate rather than abstain.
